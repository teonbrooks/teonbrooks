# Teon L Brooks, PhD

*he/him/Dr.*

## Short Bio

Teon L. Brooks, Ph.D is the co-founder of the nonprofit, [Gotham Data Clinic](https://gothamdataclinic.org) and a data scientist working with the [Brain & AI team](https://kingjr.github.io/images/team.png) at Meta FAIR.

Dr. Brooks has expertise in data science, open-source software development, neuroimaging, and neuroinformatics.
His research background in psycholinguistics, cognitive science, and data science education.

Outside of research, Teon has also been working a new project, [passports.social](https://passports.social) (pre-alpha), a new [open social app](https://atmosphereaccount.com) focused on life logging and discovery.

He posts on Bluesky 🦋 at [@teonbrooks.com](https://bsky.app/profile/teonbrooks.com).
