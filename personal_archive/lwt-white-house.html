Teon Brooks

Mozilla Science Fellow

Stanford-Mozilla Science Lab

San Francisco

Teon Brooks, PhD completed his doctorate in cognitive neuroscience at New York University. His research focused on understanding how the brain coordinates eye movements during reading. He is currently a Mozilla Science Fellow at Stanford University where he advocates for open science practices and works on developing open-source tools to allow fellow researchers to easily run and freely share their experiments.
